Game Controls

Movement Controls

  • W or Z - Jump
  • A or Q - Move Left
  • D - Move Right
  • S - Crouch

Combat Controls

  • K - Shoot

Game Controls

  • H - Start Game
  • T - Reset Game

Game Mechanics

  • Health: Both player and NPCs have 100 HP
  • Damage: Each bullet hit deals 50 damage
  • Health Bars: Red bars above characters show current health
  • NPCs: Will be removed when defeated
